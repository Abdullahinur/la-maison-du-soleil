La Maison Du Soleil

"Where the sun goes home"

Offering a garden with a terrace and barbecue facilities, La Maison du Soleil is located in Brussels. Free WiFi access is available. Brussels Airport is 6 km away. Each room here will provide you with air conditioning. Complete with a coffee machine, the dining area also has a dining table. Featuring a shower, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes. The rooms offer a garden view. Extras include a sofa, a desk and ironing facilities.
